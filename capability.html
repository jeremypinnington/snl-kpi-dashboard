Proposed additions to the FY KPI list

Stash + Lode has maintained high rates of success across Safety, Capability and Human Resources — in most measures performing well ahead of comparable Australian transport, logistics and removals businesses. These metrics are strong, board-ready measures and are recommended as positive additions to the Leadership Team KPI list for FY 2026/27.
