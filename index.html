Safety, Capability & HR

Ahead of industry

In one of Australia's highest-risk sectors, S+L recorded zero reportable incidents, a single LTI and one workers comp claim — well below sector norms. Office and driver retention both sit at 100% against a ~84% national average, and every contracted crew member received an engagement and salary review. Proposed as KPI additions for FY 26/27.
