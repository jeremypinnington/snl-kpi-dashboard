Key positive market indicators

Research & our experience shows key positive market indicators include:
Demographics

High owner-occupancy and family households

Mobility

High rates of people moving into or within the area

Rental market

Tight rental vacancy rates — < 2% rental availability

Construction

New housing estates and renovations

Income

Higher disposable incomes

Housing stock

Limited on-site storage in newer homes or apartments

Key client profiles

35%

25%

12%

10%

7%

Approximate percentages. Housing-transition clients (settlement and rental gaps) account for ~60% of all MSU clients.
